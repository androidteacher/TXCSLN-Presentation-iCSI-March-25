The Before Times

Before AI, the curriculum writing process was -extremely- time consuming.

Example:

  • We have students participate in 'live fire' activities at the beginning of many class periods.
  • Students act as penetration testers (competitively) to see who can be the first to access protected resources on a target machine set up by the instructor.
  • These activities are highly engaging and a hallmark of our program.

Pre-Artificial Intelligence, an activity that would take a group of students 20 minutes to complete would frequently take the instructor 3-4 hours of development time.

The Institute of CyberSecurity and Innovation. (NorthEast ISD. San Antonio, TX)
